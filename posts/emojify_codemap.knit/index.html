A lookup table of encodings for Hugo’s emojify function

Hugo’s emojify function is a quick and easy way to add emoji to your static site. However, the cheat sheet linked at gohugo.io is out of date, and only includes a subset of the emoji available through emojify. Here, I’ve parsed the most recent emojify gloang codemap (last updated 30 Dec 2020) in order to create a better lookup table for emojify users.

If you’d prefer, you can access the complete list of encodings as a text file.
